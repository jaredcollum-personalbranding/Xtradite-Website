What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help sites function correctly and, in some cases, help site owners understand how visitors use the site.

Cookies On This Site

This site itself does not set marketing or tracking cookies directly. It stores one piece of data in your browser's local storage (not a cookie) — an anonymous visitor token used to securely read live content from our Wix backend. This token doesn't identify you personally and isn't used for tracking or advertising.

Third-Party Services

If you submit the contact form, that submission is processed by Wix's Forms platform, which may set its own cookies as part of its infrastructure. See Wix's privacy and cookie policy for details.

Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality.
