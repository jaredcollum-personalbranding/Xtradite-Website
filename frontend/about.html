The Founder

Fifteen years of hands-on delivery, one consultancy built the same way.

I'm Jared Collum. Before founding Xtradite Digital in December 2024, I spent over a decade as an embedded operator inside retail, ecommerce, subscription and travel businesses — not an external advisor, but the person actually owning the growth P&L: acquisition, retention, CAC, LTV and payback. That includes scaling Club L London's DTC growth engine to a ~£40M+ run-rate on a multi-million-pound budget, leading WoolOvers through a COVID-era DTC pivot, and building enterprise measurement infrastructure for brands like Castore and Sweaty Betty at Fabric Analytics. Across that time I've influenced more than £50M in revenue and delivered 100+ projects for small and medium businesses globally.

Xtradite Digital is a natural continuation of that, not a reinvention: the same hands-on approach, offered as a consultancy rather than a full-time role. I'm UK based (Brighton) and work across every timezone. I also still hold an active operating role — Head of Ecommerce & Performance Growth at PROMIXX — because staying close to a live growth P&L, not just consulting from the sidelines, is exactly what keeps Xtradite Digital's advice grounded in what actually works right now.

How I Work

Five principles, each backed by a real engagement.

I embed, not just advise

Xtradite Digital doesn't hand over a strategy deck and disappear. GenderGP's community platform and Old Spike Roastery's subscription rebuild were both scoped through to a live result — not a handoff after the recommendations.

I test before I recommend

Per-Scent's testing programme ran four experiments a month, taking product impression share from 32% to 98% in four weeks. Evidence comes before conviction.

I protect margin, not just headline growth

At PROMIXX, blended ad cost (TACoS) hit a 15-month low while net margin reached 26% — and the Pursuit 2026 range launched at a sub-17% ACoS. Growth that doesn't cost margin to get.

I use AI as a lever, not a headline

Claude, Make and Zapier are part of how Xtradite Digital actually runs — turning messy backlogs into structured systems fast, the same way Old Spike Roastery's and Serious Tissues' lifecycle rebuilds got done.

I finish what I start

GenderGP's community platform went live. Serious Tissues' subscription lifecycle rebuild shipped in full. Club L London's 50-plus person team got a complete restructure, not a partial fix. Engagements end at a working result, not a recommendation.
