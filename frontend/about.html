How I Work

Five principles, each backed by a real engagement.

I embed, not just advise

Xtradite Digital doesn't hand over a strategy deck and disappear. On PROMIXX's operations rebuild and GenderGP's community platform, the work was scoped through to a live result — not a handoff after the recommendations.

I test before I recommend

Per-Scent's CRO programme ran four structured tests a month for six months before any permanent merchandising change shipped. Evidence comes before conviction.

I protect margin, not just headline growth

PROMIXX's FY27 Amazon growth plan was built with margin-protective mechanisms from the outset — per-line ad ceilings and a net-margin kill-switch — not growth at any cost.

I use AI as a lever, not a headline

PROMIXX's operations channel went from an unowned Slack thread to a structured, AI-assisted project-management system in around two weeks — AI used to process-mine a real backlog, not bolted on for the sake of it.

I finish what I start

GenderGP's community platform went live. Club L London's 24-person service team got a full restructure, not a partial fix. Engagements end at a working result, not a recommendation.
